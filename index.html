Kuiper Cheatsheet

Kuiper?

Kuiper (rhymes with hyper) is named after the Kuiper belt, the astroid belt that contains Pluto. Her birthday is ?? April, 2021.

She's an easy-going, loving little girl who will show affection to anyone who plays with her. She can be a bit shy/reserved at first, but once she comes out of her shell, she shows just how much of a little goofball she is.

Notes

  • The autofeeder keeps her from waking you up for food, but she will meow and meow when it's dinner time. I try to feed her at the same time every day. If she's hungry before dinnertime & starts begging, I remind her that she has dry food to eat.
  • Every once in a while she throws up from eating dinner too quickly. It's rare, but if it happens, I give her dinner in two batches, spaced out 30 minutes.

Behavior

Approaching

  • She is very wary of people trying to pick/snatch her up, so I usually advice folks to have one hand behind their back when they go to meet her.
  • In the beginning, she prefers just a finger or two, but quickly gets adjusted to a fuller petting experience.
  • Leaning over her or carrying items above her may make her dart.

Litter

  • I have supplied a mat along with an enclosed litterbox. The box has four snaps that come undone to allow easier access to the inside for scooping.
  • Along with the litterbox, I have a LitterChamp device for easy litter removal. Simply use your foot or knee to open it and scoop the litter in. It contains the smell and can hold quite a bit (I've never timed it out, but I'd guess at least a week or two). There are extra bags in a small box.
